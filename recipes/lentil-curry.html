Lentil Curry

Description

This Vegan Red Lentil Curry is a fan favorite and the BEST lentil curry you will ever try! It is easy and quick, making it a perfect weeknight dinner! Plus, it is creamy and indulgent but made with wholesome vegan ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Garlic-ginger paste
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Almond butter
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Curry powder
  • Red lentils
  • Full fat coconut milk
  • Vegetable broth
  • Neutral flavored oil

Steps

  1. Cook oil in pot until shimmering and then add ginger-garlic paste and spices. Give this a stir while it sautes.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. You can wash your lentils while this simmers and then add them in as well. Let simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add coconut milk and stir in 2-3 tablespoons of almond butter. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
