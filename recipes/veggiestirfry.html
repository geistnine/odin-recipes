Lazy Vegetable Stirfry

Description

This is a really easy, dirt cheap and customizable stir fry you can put together in a few minutes. You can make it as lame or as bougie as you like by swapping individual ingredients for fresher, higher quality ones.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Asian Vegetables
  • Kung Pao Stir Fry Sauce
  • Rice or Noodles
  • Sesame Oil
  • Optionally, tofu or other meat substitute

Steps

  1. Cover wok or saute pan with a light coat of sesame oil on high heat.
  2. Add vegetables first. Season well with salt and pepper. Saute for 7 minutes or so.
  3. If adding meat substitute or noodles, add them now. Either way, add kung pao sauce to the vegetables and coat. Saute for another 5 minutes.
  4. Serve as is or over rice.
